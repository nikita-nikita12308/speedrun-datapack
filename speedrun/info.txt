Speedrun Datapack for Minecraft
===============================

This datapack is designed to enhance the Minecraft speedrun experience.

Features:
- Bossbars for blaze rod and ender pearl tracking
- Progress goals in Nether, Overworld
- Coordinate display for Bastion, Fortress, and Portal
- Timer system and player death tracking

Created by: 9_24 group
GitHub: https://github.com/nikita-nikita12308/speedrun-datapack

To install:
1. Copy this folder into `.minecraft/saves/[your_world]/datapacks/`
2. Run /reload and follow in-game instructions
